Previous Award-Winning Projects

I've been fortunate to have my work honored with the 2012 Cronkite Award, the highest award given for political reporting in broadcast news. I've also earned a national Telly and an Emmy award for my work as a broadcast journalist.

Cronkite Award

I was the primary and only producer for NWCN's 2012 Cronkite Award winning politics coverage, which including more than two-dozen 30-minute specials, and and seven consecutive hours of coverage on election night. The Norman Lear Center noted the innovative use of social media and interactivity as a highlight of the NWCN submission.

Telly Award

This NWCN special report, video game industry leaders explain why the line between those who call themselves "gamers" and those who don't has disappeared. Accomplished by maximizing the efforts of an extremely limited number of crew members, while personally taking on writing, producing, guest booking, and video, graphic, and audio coordination.

Emmy Award

I was honored to earn the 2007 Northwest Regional Emmy Award for best midday/morning newscast for my work at KOMO 4 News. During the next seven years, I would earn several more nominations as well.

NATAS NW Executive Board

I volunteer as an executive Boardmember for the NATAS Northwest Region, serving as chair of the High School and College Awards Committee. Our goal is to promote the excellence in achievement by future journalists from across the region. I am also the primary presenter of the awards during Emmy night.
